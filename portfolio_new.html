Wayfinding & Persuasive Design - A

Development of a prototype for the Persuasive Technology 2020 conference using Axure. The goal was to facilitate wayfinding at the Aalborg University Create building by implementing persuasive design elements in the app and nudging in the physical space. Human-Robot-Interaction (HRI)

This section is dedicated to Human-Robot-Interaction.



Bachelor Thesis: Interaction between humans & socially assistive robots - A

Informing HRI from HHI. EMCA analysis of HHI between a brain impared person and her assistant to understand the interactional organisation of negotiation strategies during shopping. Based upon the analysis a dialog model for the proposed interaction with a socially assivite robot was created.
