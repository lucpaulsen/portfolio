Bachelor projects

This section is dedicated to projects from my bachelor in Communication and Digital Media (Information Studies) from Aalborg University (2017 - 2020)


Bachelor Thesis: Interaction between humans & socially assistive robots - A

Informing HRI from HHI. EMCA analysis of HHI between a brain impared person and her assistant to understand the interactional organisation of negotiation strategies during shopping. Based upon the analysis a dialog model for the proposed interaction with a socially assivite robot was created.

Read More

BA5: Wayfinding & Persuasive Design - A

Development of a prototype for the Persuasive Technology 2020 conference using Axure. The goal was to facilitate wayfinding at the Aalborg University Create building by implementing persuasive design elements in the app and nudging in the physical space.

Read More

BA5: ICT, Interaction & Organisation - B

Analysis based upon Activity Theory to understand the organisational change caused by the implementation of Scan & Go in a local Netto supermarket. Hierichally structured analysis conducting usability testing on the micro-level, interviewing on meso-level and analysis of contradictions on macro-level
